FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
================================================================================

Q: Will ELM327 void my warranty?
A: No. OBD-II port is standard, reading data doesn't void warranty.

Q: Can ELM327 damage my car?
A: No. It only reads data, cannot write to ECU or cause damage.

Q: Will all my test scripts work with ELM327?
A: Yes! test_nisscom_device.py will work perfectly with ELM327.

Q: Do I need special drivers?
A: Most ELM327 adapters work automatically on Windows 10/11.
   Some need CH340 or FTDI driver (Windows installs automatically).

Q: Bluetooth or USB?
A: USB is more reliable. Bluetooth is convenient for phone use.

Q: Can I use it while driving?
A: Yes, but don't look at computer while driving. Use passenger or
   mount phone with Torque app.

Q: Will it drain my battery?
A: No. OBD port only powers on with ignition. Unplug when not in use.

Q: Can it read airbag codes?
A: No. Standard OBD-II doesn't include airbag system.
   Need Consult-II adapter for that.

Q: What about transmission codes?
A: Some transmission codes yes (engine-related), but limited.
   Full transmission diagnostics needs Consult-II.
