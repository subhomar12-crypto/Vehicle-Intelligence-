## Your Data Rights

### You Can Always:

- **View** your data in the app
- **Export** your data (Settings > Privacy > Export)
- **Delete** your data (Settings > Privacy > Delete)
- **Disable** optional collection (Settings > Privacy)
- **Revoke** guardian access (Settings > Guardian)

### How to Exercise Rights:

1. **In-App:** Settings > Privacy > Data Management
2. **Email:** privacy@previlium.com
3. **Response:** Within 30 days

## Data Security

Your data is protected by:

- **Encryption** - All data encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Secure Authentication** - Passwords hashed, tokens protected
- **Access Controls** - Only authorized access to your data
- **Regular Audits** - We test our security regularly

## AI Predictions & Your Data

### How AI Uses Your Data:

1. Your vehicle's sensor data is analyzed by our AI models
2. Predictions are generated about potential issues
3. You receive alerts about predicted problems

### Important Notes:

- AI predictions are **estimates, not guarantees**
- We use **anonymized aggregate data** to train models
- Your individual data improves **your vehicle's** predictions
- You can opt out of contributing to model training
